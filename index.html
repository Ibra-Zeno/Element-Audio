About me

Shuma Begum

I am a London based audio artist, with a Bangladeshi background. I am passionate about bringing art to life through various sound disciplines. I strive to represent women and ethnic minorities in the audio industry. I took a leap of faith when I decided to study Music and Sound Design at university. A career in an artistic role was incomprehensible in upbringing, but I decided to create my own path in the professional world. I consider myself an amorphous person, not limited in the roles I want to pursue in life, thus leading to the birth of Element Audio. I wanted to offer a variety of services to clients, with the aim of helping people deliver content that fulfils their artistic vision.

My Portfolio

Hello, and welcome to my online portfolio! As an audio professional, I take pride in showcasing my various projects and experiences in sound design, music production, and live performance.

First up, you'll find a selection of foley projects that I've worked on. From creating realistic footsteps to crafting unique sound effects for film and TV, foley work is all about creating a world of sound that complements the visuals on screen. I've had the pleasure of working on several foley projects, and I'm excited to share some of my favorites with you.

Next, you'll find a collection of songs that I've produced and composed. These tracks showcase my ability to work in a variety of genres, from electronic to acoustic, and my skills as a songwriter and arranger. I'm always experimenting with new sounds and techniques, and I believe these tracks demonstrate my passion for music and my commitment to creating something unique and engaging.

If you're interested in audiovisual projects, you'll find several clips that I've worked on, ranging from short films to experimental installations. These projects allow me to explore the intersection of sound and image, and to create immersive experiences that engage both the eyes and ears.

Of course, no portfolio would be complete without a mixing project. Mixing is a crucial part of the production process, and I pride myself on my ability to take a raw recording and transform it into a polished, professional track. Whether it's a song or a piece of dialogue, I'm always striving to bring out the best in every element of a mix.

Finally, I invite you to check out my live performance with singing bowls. This unique instrument has a rich history and a beautiful sound, and I've had the pleasure of incorporating it into my live shows. My performance combines singing bowls with other instruments and effects, creating a meditative and immersive experience for the audience.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I'm excited to share my work with you, and I hope it inspires you to explore the world of sound and music!
